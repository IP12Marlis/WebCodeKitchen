Roman numerals converter

From roman numerals to arabic numbers

Please enter a roman numerals. Below you find the rules to combine roman numerals.

  • In the beginning there was no symbol for zero. Later they have taken N for Nulla (latin word for none) and Nihil (latin word for nothing)
  • The basic symbols for numbers power of ten are: I = 1, X = 10, C = 100, M = 1000. You must combine maximal three of them. (e.g. XXX = 30)
  • The symbols between the basic symbols for numbers five times numbers power of ten are: V = 5, L = 50, D = 500. You must combine only one of them. (e.g. XVII = 17)
  • The values of the symbols are added to find the arabic number. (e.g. VI = 6)
  • If a symbol with a smaller value is on the left side of another symbol, it has to be subtracted from the symbol on the right. (e.g. IV = 4)
